History

Patrick Goldfarb (1914-2008) grew up in Brooklyn. As a young adult, he moved to Seattle to start his cookie business. He found that the flavors of the fresh salmon of the Pacific Northwest went well with his cookies, and he created the salmon cookie. The locals loved it so much, that he opened locations around the world.
